About Us

Founded in 2015 by pastry chef Julia Simmons, Sweet Delights Bakery has grown from small, local shop to a beloved community landmark. Julia's dream was to create a place where people could come together to enjoy delicious baked goods made with love and care. Today, Sweet Delights Bakery is known for its artisanal breads, decadent pastries, and custom cakes that are perfect for any occasion.
