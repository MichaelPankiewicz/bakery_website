Founded in 2015 by pastry chef Julia Simmons, Sweet Delights Bakery has grown from small, local shop to a beloved community landmark. Today, Sweet Delights Bakery is known for its artisanal breads, decadent pastries, and custom cakes that are perfect for any occasion.
